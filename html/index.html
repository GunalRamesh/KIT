Since Year 2007

We are KIT

Institute of Technology was established in the year 2007, by Dr.R. Vasantha Kumar and is accredited by AICTE, NAAC and NBA and takes its affiliation from the state technical university of Tamil Nadu, Anna University (AU). It offers B.E and B.Tech courses.

The Karpagam Institute Of Technology (KIT) is one of the branches of Karpagam Institutions, which were referred to as KI's.

Our Institution has got an excellent infrastructure and a peaceful atmosphere for the benefits of the students to excel in studies.
